Notable Sites

The Democratic People's Republic of Korea has many notable sites, in and outside of Pyongyang!

Tourism to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is great fun for all workers and their families!

  • Recently Completed Ryugyong Hotel!

    The Ryugyong hotel was completed in 2002 by the glorious General Secretary of the Workers' Party, Kim Jong-il.

  • Kim Il-sung Square

    This great square in the center of Pyongyang was finished in 1954 and serves as a testament to the ideals of the workers!

    It glorifies the memory of the Eternal President Kim Il-sung

  • Juche Tower

    Designed by the glorious leader Kim Jong-il and finished in 1982, the Juche Tower commemorates the Eternal President Kim Il-sung's 70th birthday.

  • Paektu Mountain

    This mountain is the most beautiful in the world! The sacred mountain of the revolution!
